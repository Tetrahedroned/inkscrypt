After You Download

01
Print the letter. Do not send digitally. A physical letter bypasses automated systems and demands human processing.
02
Sign it with a pen on the signature line. Your wet ink signature forces a human being to process this — not an automated OCR bot.
03
Mail via USPS Certified Mail with return receipt requested (Form 3800). Keep the green card. That receipt is your proof of delivery.
04
Keep a copy of everything — the letter, envelope, tracking number, return receipt. Document every step.
05
File a CFPB complaint at consumerfinance.gov/complaint if unresolved. Free. Creates a federal record.
